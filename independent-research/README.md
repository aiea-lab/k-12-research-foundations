# Independent Research Opportunities in the AIEA Lab for K-12 students

For K-12 students that are not interested in the active projects, they
can define their own project over the summer.  

Here’s how it works:

-   You define your own project: This should be an original idea or a
    continuation of an existing project.
-   You can work solo or in a small team: Teams of 2-4 students are
    welcome. If you're forming a team, make sure each member contributes
    and is listed in your project doc.
-   Share your proposal: Write a short project description in a Google
    Doc and share it with me. You will duplicate this [template](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KmjISfUkNmohjMU0WAn2c8Va1qbSxCO7kI0P3Qi-q2c/edit?usp=sharing) and share
    it with me (lgilpin@ucsc.edu).
